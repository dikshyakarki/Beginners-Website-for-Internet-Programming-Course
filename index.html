About

Tibetan Handicrafts and Bead Store is a family owned business started in 1995 with the purpose of delivering handmade home decors, thanka, sculptures and custom designed jewelries. All our products are environment friendly. We make the products in the shop itself with the help of incredible artisans (mostly women) based on Nepal and Tibet. Our main purpose is to provide beautiful and indigenous decors and jewelries to you with the sole purpose of preserving the heritages and local arts. With your help, we have been able to provide employment opportunities to few people.

Products

Mandala

The meaning of mandala comes from Sanskrit meaning "circle." Even though it may be dominated by squares or triangles, a mandala has a concentric structure. Mandalas offer balancing visual elements, symbolizing unity and harmony. The goal of the mandala is to serve as a tool on our spiritual journey as it symbolizes cosmic and psychic order.

Bracelets and Anklets

For the Nepalese jewelry is an important vehicle of meanings and an essential tool to protect themselves from external evils and to connect to positive energies; therefore, jewels are very popular and often are worn in great quantities, one above the other, in a magnificent and chaotic demonstration of abundance and protection from evil forces.

Woolen Products

We manufacture every kind of woolen outfits like Woolen Jackets, Woolen Scrafs, Woolen Hats, Woolen Sweaters woolen pooncho, Earflaps and Woolen socks from best wool of mountain Yak and Goat. For exporting woolen products from Nepal in wholesale and retail prices, contact us. According to your design and color we do manufacture too.

Statues

The metal statues or the meatal crafts are highly know in Nepal. The cating of bronze, brass and copper statuary is Nepal's oldest living craft tradition, dating back at least 1300 years. Modern metal crafts are develop finde mode based on antique metal work style. Metal Statues are of Buddhist deities in Tibetan style, although there are Newari style of Buddhist and Hindu images.
